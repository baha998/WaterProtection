## Our Pledge
We commit to maintaining a respectful, safe, and inclusive environment for all contributors regardless of gender, age, religion, nationality, or experience level.

## Our Standards
Positive behavior includes:
- respectful communication  
- constructive feedback  
- helping beginners  
- valuing different perspectives  

Unacceptable behavior includes:
- harassment, insults, or hate speech  
- discrimination  
- publishing private information  
- toxic or aggressive behavior  

## Enforcement

The team will review the issue and take appropriate action.
